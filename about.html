Company Overview

Quez Global Music Publishing (QGM) is an independent music publishing company focused on intellectual property ownership, royalty maximization, and global licensing opportunities.

As an ASCAP affiliated publisher, QGM administers performance royalties, mechanical royalties, and sync placements while maintaining 100% controlled catalog transparency and compliance standards.

Mission

Our mission is to empower songwriters and producers with structured publishing infrastructure that protects ownership, increases revenue streams, and positions compositions for long-term asset growth.

Vision

QGM aims to become a premier independent urban publishing house bridging creative culture with executive-level business strategy. We believe in transforming music into scalable intellectual property.

Core Services

  • Global Performance Royalty Collection (ASCAP)
  • Mechanical Royalty Administration
  • Sync Licensing Representation
  • Metadata & Copyright Registration
  • Music Supervisor & Creative Pitching
  • Catalog Strategy & Monetization Planning

Founder & Creative Direction

QGM was founded by RocBoi Quez, an Atlanta-based recording artist and songwriter focused on ownership, catalog control, and independent wealth building through music.

The company operates with a “Street Executive” philosophy — blending authentic cultural influence with disciplined publishing structure.

Philosophy

"Own Your Sound. Control Your Empire."

Music is not just art — it is equity. QGM exists to protect and expand that equity.
