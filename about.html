ABOUT QGM

Where Independent Hip-Hop Meets Major-Level Publishing

OUR MISSION

Quez Global Music Publishing (QGM) exists to provide independent artists — starting with RocBoi Quez — with professional-grade publishing services that maximize ownership, global performance royalties, and synchronization opportunities. Our mission is to merge **street-level authenticity** with **major-label precision**.

OUR VISION

To establish a **world-class hip-hop publishing empire** that sets the standard for independent music ownership. QGM empowers artists by controlling their catalog, securing sync placements, and maintaining full transparency with industry-standard administration through ASCAP.

WHAT WE DO

QGM handles every aspect of music publishing for RocBoi Quez, including: - Global performance rights management - Mechanical and synchronization licensing - Digital and traditional royalty collection - Strategic placement in Film, TV, gaming, commercials, and social media campaigns We ensure every track is monetized, protected, and positioned for maximum industry impact.

OUR COMMITMENT

At QGM, independence is not an obstacle — it’s our advantage. We operate with the **precision of a corporate publisher** while preserving the authenticity, grit, and identity of Atlanta hip-hop culture.
