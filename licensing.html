Rights & Clearance

QGM provides both master and publishing clearance coordination when applicable. For controlled compositions, we offer:

  • Exclusive Sync Licenses
  • Non-Exclusive Sync Licenses
  • Territory-Specific Agreements
  • Term-Based & Perpetual Usage Options
  • Custom Negotiated License Structures

All agreements are structured to ensure compliance with ASCAP performance reporting and international royalty collection standards.

Licensing Process

  1. Submit project brief & usage details
  2. Receive curated music options (if requested)
  3. Negotiate licensing terms & budget
  4. Execute agreement & deliver final assets
  5. Cue sheet & royalty tracking confirmation

Standard response time: 24–48 hours. Expedited clearance available for time-sensitive productions.
