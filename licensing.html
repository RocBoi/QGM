GLOBAL LICENSING & SYNC

Quez Global Music Publishing (QGM) provides full publishing rights, sync clearance, and licensing for all media projects worldwide.

Our Licensing Services

QGM Publishing specializes in professional licensing for independent and commercial projects. We handle:

  • Film and Television placements
  • Streaming platforms and web content
  • Video game soundtracks
  • Commercial and advertising campaigns
  • Podcasts and digital media productions

All submissions are reviewed by our Rights Administration team to ensure accurate royalty collection, legal compliance, and timely clearance. We provide exclusive and non-exclusive licensing agreements depending on the project scope.

How to Submit Your Request

Please submit all licensing inquiries to our dedicated email: licensing@rocboiquez.online

Include the following details for faster processing:

  • Project name and description
  • Media type (film, TV, commercial, game, digital)
  • Expected usage duration
  • Territory / market
  • Budget or licensing range

QGM responds within 24–48 hours to ensure your project stays on schedule. Our goal is to make licensing simple, transparent, and efficient while maintaining full control of RocBoi Quez’s catalog and rights.
